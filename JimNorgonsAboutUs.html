About Us

History

We are a family-run business who loves sharing our favorite family recipes with others. We always prioritize members of the local community in hiring our staff. 5% of our profits go to a scholarship fund for underprivileged but talented youth in our neighborhood.

Locations

Because we are still a new business, we unfortunately only have one branch, and that is at Narra avenue, Mapayapa Village 3, barangay Pasong Tamo, Quezon City. But the good news is, thanks to our ever-growing crowd of supporters, we wills soon be opening new branches across the Philippines! Hopefully in the next 10 years, we'll be an international establishment.
